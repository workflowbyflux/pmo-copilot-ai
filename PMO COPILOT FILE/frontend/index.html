Why Multi-Agent PMO?

Conventional single-prompt chat systems often suffer from context dilution, drift, and lack of specialized knowledge when designing full-scale projects.

PMO Copilot AI solves this by dividing execution into a multi-role team. By separating concerns between a Business Analyst, Project Manager, Risk Manager, and Release Manager, the platform produces structured deliverables that are consistent, audit-logged, and validated against strict schemas.
